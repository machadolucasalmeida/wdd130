History

Whitewater Rafting Adventures was born in 1995 when two river guides, Mark and Sarah, turned their passion for rapids into a business. Starting with just one raft and a love for adventure, they built a company dedicated to thrilling—yet safe—whitewater experiences.

Over the years, we’ve guided thousands down roaring rivers, trained expert guides, and earned a reputation for unforgettable trips. Today, we’re still family-owned, still obsessed with the rush of the water, and still committed to sharing the best of the wild with you.
